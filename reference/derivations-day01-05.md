# Important Derivations — Week 1 Day 1 to Day 5

## 1. Inverse of Linear Function

$$
y=ax+b\Rightarrow x=\frac{y-b}{a}
$$

$$
\boxed{f^{-1}(x)=\frac{x-b}{a}}
$$

## 2. Dimension of $G$

$$
F=\frac{Gm_1m_2}{r^2}
\Rightarrow
G=\frac{Fr^2}{m_1m_2}
$$

$$
\boxed{[G]=M^{-1}L^3T^{-2}}
$$

## 3. Error Propagation

For $Q=x^ay^bz^c$:

$$
\ln Q=a\ln x+b\ln y+c\ln z
$$

$$
\boxed{\frac{\Delta Q}{Q}=|a|\frac{\Delta x}{x}+|b|\frac{\Delta y}{y}+|c|\frac{\Delta z}{z}}
$$

## 4. Kinematic Equation

$$
a=\frac{dv}{dt}=\frac{dv}{dx}\frac{dx}{dt}=v\frac{dv}{dx}
$$

$$
v\,dv=a\,dx
$$

$$
\boxed{v^2=u^2+2as}
$$

## 5. Projectile Range

$$
T=\frac{2u\sin\theta}{g}
$$

$$
R=u\cos\theta\cdot T
$$

$$
\boxed{R=\frac{u^2\sin2\theta}{g}}
$$

## 6. Chain Rule

$$
y=f(u),\quad u=g(x)
$$

$$
\boxed{\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{dy}{du}\frac{du}{dx}}
$$

## 7. Work-Energy Theorem

$$
F=ma,
\qquad a=v\frac{dv}{dx}
$$

$$
Fdx=mvdv
$$

$$
\boxed{W=\Delta K}
$$

## 8. Conservative Force-Potential Relation

$$
W_{cons}=-\Delta U
$$

$$
Fdx=-dU
$$

$$
\boxed{F=-\frac{dU}{dx}}
$$

## 9. Spring Potential

$$
F=-kx=-\frac{dU}{dx}
$$

$$
\boxed{U=\frac12kx^2}
$$

## 10. Bond Order

$$
\boxed{BO=\frac{N_b-N_a}{2}}
$$
